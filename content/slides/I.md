## 时间尺度下飞速进展

- Momentum-based GD:

    $$
    \theta_{t} \leftarrow \theta_{t-1} - \eta \cdot \textcolor{blue}{\bm{m}_t}, \: \bm{m}_t \leftarrow \mu \cdot \bm{m}_{t-1} + \bm{g}_{t}

    \bm{m}_t \leftarrow \beta_1 \cdot \bm{m}_{t-1} + (1 - \beta_1) \cdot \bm{g}_t, \\

    $$
